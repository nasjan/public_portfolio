Mentalink

Matching service for finding the right therapist.

Overview

Finding and comparing therapists is surprisingly hard. Mentalink was designed to make that easier by lowering the barrier to start therapy and speeding up the browsing process. The concept includes a swipe deck for quick browsing, clear therapist profiles, and a support area called Mentaspace where users can access resources and community support.

View prototype

Project facts

Course: Innovointi ja prototyypin rakentaminen (DIG002AS2A-3011)

School: Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences

Timeline: About 8 weeks in 2024

Team: Jan Nässi, Miikka Saukkonen

My role: Visual design lead, UI hierarchy, logo design

Goal: Create a simple, human-centered way for people to find therapists quickly and feel confident about their choice.

Methods: Brainstorming, Miro mapping, low-fi prototype in Framer, high-fi prototype in Figma, quick usability tests with 8 people via Microsoft Forms

Tools: Figma, Framer, Miro, Microsoft Forms, Adobe Illustrator

What I delivered: High-fidelity prototype, logo, core user flows, test summary

Results:

  • 7 out of 8 testers found the UI very clear
  • 7 out of 8 strongly liked the visual style
  • 6 out of 8 said they would recommend the product
