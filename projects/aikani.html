Aikani

Senior-first companion for daily planning, voice-assisted guidance, and local activity discovery.

Overview

Aikani is a concept for independent seniors in Finland. It combines a clear, high-contrast calendar, a voice-controlled assistant, and a community events portal to support routines and reduce isolation.

Video Presentation

60-second pitch video summarizing one user day.

Project facts

Course: DIG005AS2A-3004 — DigiTuote (Digital Product)

Institution: Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences

Timeframe: ~3 weeks (2024)

Team: Jan Nässi, Mirka, Alexandra, Laura, Noora

My role: Video generation; sound design; script co-writing

Objective: Learn end-to-end digital product concepting and create a senior-first companion concept combining calendar, voice assistance, and community events; produce an AI-assisted concept ad

Methods: Concept ideation in a team; lightweight research; six personas and empathy mapping; JTBD; wireframing; selected hi-fi screens; rapid peer feedback; ad concept planning

Tools: Figma; Adobe Illustrator; Sora (video); Suno (music); Speechify (TTS)

Deliverables: Concept summary; personas and JTBD; user flows and wireframes; selected hi-fi screens; logo and visual identity; 60-second promo video; pitch deck

Note: Group project. Everyone contributed to planning and execution.
