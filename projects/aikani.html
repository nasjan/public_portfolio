Aikani

A companion app for seniors that helps with daily planning, voice-assisted guidance, and finding local activities.

Overview

Aikani is designed for independent seniors in Finland who want to stay organized and connected. It brings together a simple calendar with high contrast, a voice assistant that actually helps, and a way to discover community events nearby. The goal is to support daily routines and reduce feelings of isolation.

Project facts

Course: DIG005AS2A-3004, DigiTuote (Digital Product)

School: Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences

Timeline: About 6 weeks in 2025

Team: Jan Nässi, Mirka, Alexandra, Laura, Noora

My role: Video generation, sound design, script co-writing

Goal: Learn the full process of creating a digital product concept and build something specifically for seniors that combines calendar, voice help, and community events. Then make a promo video using AI tools.

Methods: Team brainstorming and concept development, lightweight research, created six personas and empathy maps, Jobs To Be Done framework, wireframing, selected high-fidelity screens, quick peer feedback sessions, ad planning

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Sora for video, Suno for music, Speechify for voice

What we delivered: Concept summary, personas and JTBD analysis, user flows and wireframes, selected hi-fi screens, logo and visual identity, 60-second promo video, pitch deck

Note: This was a group project. Everyone pitched in on planning and execution.
