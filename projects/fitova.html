Fitova

Research • Wireframes • Prototype

Overview

Problem: People struggle to find suitable therapy options quickly.

Context: Concept project.

My role: UX design, research, prototyping.

Timeline: 4 weeks.

Objectives & Success

  • Guide users to relevant providers.
  • Reduce decision friction.
  • Validate with quick usability tests.

Research

Interviews and desk research surfaced confusion around therapy types and availability.

Synthesis

Key insights mapped to user needs and requirements using lightweight personas and JTBD.

Ideation

Explored flows and wireframes to simplify provider selection.

Design

Iterated from low to higher fidelity screens focusing on clarity and guidance.

Validation

Usability tests indicated improved confidence; small copy changes reduced errors.

Outcome

Prototype increased task completion in testing scenarios; next steps include provider filters.

Learnings

Terminology clarity matters; progressive disclosure reduced overwhelm.
