Logo Design

Four logo projects showing my process, style choices, and results.

Nosara

Instagram

Hard techno artist identity

The client wanted something rough and aggressive for her hard techno brand. We worked together in quick feedback cycles. I looked at metal band logos for inspiration, used AI to explore different texture styles, then rebuilt the winning direction from scratch in Illustrator and Photoshop.

  • Style: Harsh, textured wordmark inspired by metal aesthetics
  • Colors: Black, gray, white
  • Goal: Raw, bold, high-impact presence
  • Status: Being used on flyers and merch. T-shirts already produced. Delivered full kit with different formats and color versions.

Heppahubi.fi

Instagram

Equine care and stand-in service

They needed a clean logo that combined a helping hand with a horse head. I sketched out several ideas, got feedback from the client, and refined the chosen concept into a simple, friendly symbol that works at any size.

  • Style: Minimalist line icon with calm, balanced geometry
  • Colors: Green main version, plus black and white variant
  • Goal: Feel trustworthy and caring, stay clear even at small sizes
  • Status: Delivered and actively used. Full handover kit with variants and file formats.

Mentalink

Therapy matching concept (student project)

After we settled on the name, I designed a logo that connects mind and connection. The brain shape has a link symbol in the center to represent what the product does.

  • Style: Clean, geometric icon
  • Colors: Blue as the main color
  • Typography: Montserrat font family
  • Goal: Look professional and trustworthy
  • Status: Used throughout the course project.

Saumaton

Fictive UX and web agency - course site

For this fictional agency project, I created a modern mark built around the letter S. The continuous form inside a circle hints at seamless service delivery.

  • Style: Modern monogram mark
  • Colors: Dark gray and white
  • Goal: Professional and sleek look
  • Status: Used on the course WordPress site prototype.
