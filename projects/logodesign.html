Logo Design

Four concise logo projects. Process, style, and outcomes.

Nosara

Instagram

Hard techno artist identity

Brief: design a rough and aggressive artist logotype. We co-created in short feedback cycles. I explored metal band references and presented AI-assisted style studies, then rebuilt the chosen direction in Illustrator and Photoshop.

  • Style: harsh, textured, metal-inspired wordmark
  • Colors: black, gray, white
  • Goal: raw, bold, high-impact
  • Status: in use on flyers, digital and print. T-shirt merch produced. Delivery kit with formats and colorways.

Heppahubi.fi

Instagram

Equine care and stand-in service

Brief: create a minimalist mark combining a helping hand and a horse head. I drafted multiple options, reviewed with the client, and refined the selected direction to a simple, friendly symbol.

  • Style: minimalist line icon with calm geometry
  • Colors: black and white; green, white, gray variant
  • Goal: trust, care, clarity at small sizes
  • Status: delivered and in use. Handover kit with variants and file formats.

Mentalink

Therapy matching concept - student project

Brief: design a logo after the name decision. The idea combines mind and connection. I built a brain symbol with a central link to echo the product promise.

  • Style: clean, geometric icon
  • Colors: blue primary
  • Typography: Montserrat family
  • Goal: trust and professionalism
  • Status: used in the course project assets.

Saumaton

Fictive UX and web agency - course site

Brief: create a modern mark referencing the initial S. The symbol uses a continuous form inside a circle to suggest seamless delivery.

  • Style: modern monogram mark
  • Colors: dark gray, white
  • Goal: professional and sleek
  • Status: used on the course WordPress site prototype.
