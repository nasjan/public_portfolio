About

Approach: I'm studying Business Information Technology at Haaga-Helia with a focus on digital services and UX design. What drives my work is genuine curiosity about people and figuring out how technology can actually help them. I want to create digital experiences that feel easy and natural, not frustrating. I like mixing structured thinking with creative exploration, and I use AI tools to quickly test out new design ideas and directions.

Strengths: I bring together tech knowledge, visual design skills, and a calm way of solving problems. I spent nearly ten years in logistics, which taught me to be precise, reliable, and great at making things flow smoothly. Now I apply that same mindset to user journeys and product design. I'm always looking to improve, I enjoy learning new tools, and I believe good design starts with understanding people and ends with making things clear and simple.
